I'm an experimental particle physicist and Associate Professor in the
[Department of Physics & Astronomy](https://physics.olemiss.edu/) at the
University of Mississippi, where I also serve as Graduate Program Coordinator.
My group studies how neutrinos — the universe's most elusive particles —
change identity as they travel. I'm the Mississippi Principal Investigator
for Fermilab's [DUNE](https://www.dunescience.org/) experiment and a
long-time collaborator on [NOvA](https://novaexperiment.fnal.gov/) and
[EMPHATIC](https://emphatic.fnal.gov/).
